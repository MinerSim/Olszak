Own minigames

On our network we present to you our self-programmed minigames. We strive towards improving your experience playing other games by allowing to train certain skills while also providing you with a small range of competitive games. We are always working in the background to either make new games or improve already existing ones.

Our staff team

The most important thing is that everyone feels well on the server. Each and every member of our team is chosen carefully in order to manage an ever-growing community. Depending on the role of the staff member, they will build maps, develop plugins, help out players or punish rule-breakers.

Great servers

We try to keep our servers updated and in good shape in order to deliver a lag-free experience. Servers are currently hosted in Germany but this should not bother players from other destinations too much.

Our minigames

FastBuilder

Being our most famous mode, it allows you to improve your bridging skills. Originally invented by TROOP3R and McPlayHD in order to better their own ninja bridging, it now helps thousands of players a day to get to the opponent's beds faster. Several new bridging techniques have been invented by our community on our server and more have yet to come.

Quakecraft

Quakecraft is one of the first shooters to be implemented in Minecraft by the Hypixel Network. Equipped with a railgun your goal is to reach more kills than anyone else in a FFA mode. Originally, back in 2013 the server's only gamemode was Quakecraft for quite some time.

Slender

Slender is a well-known horror game. The goal is to survive the onslaught of the Slenderman. Eight pages are hidden at midnight and finding all of them will break the curse. The Slenderman is equipped with a bloodstained axe which he has to infuse with power by staring down his innocent victims.

BedWars

BedWars is a fully automatic version of the original map Rush. BedWars became well known thanks to the server GommeHD.net and is playable on many other servers. The goal of the game is to destroy everyone else's bed so they can no longer respawn and then kill them all.

CakeDefense

CakeDefense is a game all about eating that cake. In order to eat your opponent's cake, you have to stand on top of it. Devouring all seven pieces of the cake will prevent your enemies from further respawning and you can finally eliminate them.

Minesweeper

Minesweeper is probably the best known Windows XP system game which is a logic game with the goal of defusing a minefield without letting it explode. Stepping on a field will reveal information about how many adjacent fields are rigged to explode. With this information you can solve most minefields and successfully defuse all mines.

Snowbattle

Snowbattle delivers completely on its name. You and your friends try to eliminate your opposing team by landing successful hits with a snowball. These snowballs can be obtained by breaking layers of snow. The first team to win five rounds of snowball fights will win the game and return to their home victoriously.

Moorhuhn

Originally a CD game for Windows XP, Moorhuhn now allows you to train your bow accuracy. Equipped with a bow and arrow, your goal is to shoot as many chickens in 90 seconds as possible whilst only being able to turn around your own axis.
